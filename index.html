C&A EVENTS

Of timeless love stories, elegant gatherings, and unforgettable celebrations… At C&A Events, we specialize in bringing dream occasions to life. As a trusted wedding and event planning company in the Philippines, we offer full-service planning — from concept creation, vendor sourcing, budget and timeline management, to seamless on-the-day coordination. Whether it’s an intimate wedding, a grand celebration, or a corporate affair, our expertise lies in transforming visions into remarkable realities. Having organized destination weddings, local festivities, and participated in prestigious bridal fairs, we pride ourselves on delivering events that are not only beautiful, but also stress-free for our clients.
From curating themes and color palettes, recommending and liaising with suppliers, to ensuring every detail is refined and cohesive, we take care of every step so you can simply enjoy the moment. CONCEPT. CREATION. CURATION. CELEBRATION.


About Us

C&A Events (Charles and Anj Events Management Services) is a premier event planning company, owned and operated by Ms. Angelica Castillo and Mr. Charles Castillo. Since its inception in 2014, C&A Events has been dedicated to creating memorable experiences for all types of celebrations. What began as a small venture specializing in cake-making and kiddie party supplies has grown into a full-service event planning and coordination company, catering to a diverse range of occasions.



Our Expertise

With over a decade of experience, C&A Events has styled over 200 weddings across Luzon. We pride ourselves on creativity, attention to detail, and turning clients’ dreams into reality.

Our Journey

Our journey began with a passion for baking and a heartfelt desire to bring joy to children’s celebrations. In 2018, we proudly earned our Events Management (NCIII) certification and partnered with Weddings Beautiful Worldwide, which strengthened our expertise in creating seamless and memorable occasions. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we continue to turn visions into unforgettable experiences with creativity, professionalism, and care.
